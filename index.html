Services

Grantwriting

We build proposals from concept to submission in diverse formats including online submissions, Common Grant Applications, and state and federal RFPs. Nothing we do is cookie cutter or boilerplate: we learn your voice, tell your story, and build a compelling case for support.

Our level of involvement is catered to your specific needs and goals, whether creating a customized proposal for a particular project, funder, or RFP, or providing more generic narrative pieces that can be utilized in a variety of ways.

Research / Studies

At times, an external and objective viewpoint is valuable to determine the best course of action. Working in conjunction with your staff, we conduct in-depth prospect research, external scans, or feasibility studies for clients seeking new opportunities.

Development Strategy

We work with your team and identified stakeholders to review current development strategies and goals to devise a task-oriented plan that engages donors and attracts and retains funding.
